Film Portfolio

Asa Shepard

A collection of my favorite film projects over the years. I have enjoyed working with digital media since the early 2010s and continue to today. Most of the films here were produced during my time at LASA High School as part of LASA Film.

While I most enjoy editing and the post-production process, I also enjoy the creative aspect that comes with writing and directing which I delved into with "Earth to You" and "Everyone Shall Sit on a Little Donkey," collaborating to adapt scripts written by my classmates as well as coordinating the actors and crew and arranging shots during shooting.
